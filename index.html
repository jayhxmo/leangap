This is no ordinary camp

Leangap is not your ordinary summer program. Every year, we invite a handful of teenagers from around the world to Boston, where they embark on an intense six week journey to build real, scaleable companies.

Start your journey

Leangap helps high school students start real companies in one summer. Over 6 weeks, students will come up with ideas, build the product, and launch.

Analyze

During the first week, student entrepreneurs will pitch ideas, form teams, validate business models, and get personal mentorship from top entrepreneurs.

Build

Teams will then create real products that will enhance the lives of others with the guidance of our mentors. They will then BETA test w/ customers and reiterate.

Launch

On the last week, students will demo and pitch the product to investors. Startups will launch the product to the market, and generate real traction.
